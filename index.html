How It Works

From Raw Data to Automated Action

AISAA's four-stage intelligence pipeline transforms disconnected academic data into coordinated, automated institutional support — without manual effort.

01

Ingest & Centralise

Academic records, grades, attendance, and enrolment data flow from the university SIS into AISAA's Supabase data layer via scheduled secure exports.

02

Classify & Detect

The deterministic Risk Evaluation Engine calculates MRP status, WAM, task mark trends, and attendance thresholds for every student — updated as data changes.

03

Alert & Escalate

When any threshold is breached, targeted alerts are dispatched to the relevant stakeholder — student, faculty, advisor, or ASC — with the specific issue identified.

04

Automate & Resolve

The n8n agentic workflow engine autonomously schedules advisor meetings, syncs calendars, sends reminders, and dispatches bulk recommendation emails.

Five Stakeholder Roles

One Platform. Every Role Served.

AISAA delivers tailored, access-controlled dashboards for each of the five institutional roles — each seeing only what is relevant and authorised for their function.

Role 01 — Institutional Leadership

Head of Departments

Programme-level analytics and governance intelligence across all students, courses, and academic trends.

→Course analysis and bottleneck detection with fail rate flags
→MRP and WAM distribution monitoring across the full programme
→Top graduate rankings per course and academic year
→Quota forecast based on aggregated student recommendations
→Potential graduate identification (CP ≤ 18)
Role 02 — Academic Advising

Advisor

Comprehensive oversight of assigned students with automated meeting scheduling and intervention tracking.

→Advised student list with WAM, MRP, attendance, and alert counts
→Automated meeting scheduling via n8n with calendar conflict resolution
→Full student profile with performance, degree progress, and recommendations
→Excuse request review — approve, reject, or reassign to ASC
→Accessibility notes from ASC visible in each student's profile
Role 03 — Course Delivery

Faculty

Course-level performance monitoring, attendance management, and direct access to student support intelligence.

→Attendance recording and per-student absence tracking per course
→At-risk student alerts with task mark trend analysis
→Excuse management — approve, reject, or reassign to Advisor
→Counsellor notes and accessibility requirements from ASC
→Course analytics with weekly attendance graphs and student lookup
Role 04 — Student Support Services

Academic Success Centre

Centralised student welfare management, counsellor documentation, and support resource allocation.

→Counsellor notes creation and delivery to relevant faculty
→Reassigned excuse request review and final resolution
→At-risk student monitoring across all enrolled students
→Quota forecast and potential graduate tracking
→Data export in PDF, Excel, and CSV for governance reporting
Role 05 — Academic Journey

Student

A personalised academic intelligence dashboard that keeps students informed, proactive, and on track for graduation.

→Real-time dashboard showing MRP status, WAM, attendance, and task marks
→Visual Degree Planner with year-by-year CP tracking and prerequisites
→Subject recommendations (2–4 subjects) with graduation timeline

→Excuse submission, meeting alerts, and FAQ Chatbot via Groq AI
Platform Capabilities

Intelligent by Design. Automated by Default.

Every capability in AISAA is built around one principle: the right information reaches the right person at the right time, without manual effort.

Deterministic Risk Classification Engine

AISAA's Risk Evaluation Engine applies fixed, university-defined threshold rules to classify every student's academic standing — no machine learning, no guesswork, fully auditable.

MRP classified as Active (≥66%), Referral (50–65%), Restricted (<50%), or Excluded per policy
WAM calculated as weighted average of subject marks with classification bands and alert thresholds
Task mark trends evaluated for sharp drops, consecutive declines, and performance below course average
Attendance threshold checks per course with automated absence alerts

About Us

Built by QuadCore, for Institutions That Care

Based in Dubai, UAE

Transforming Academic Management Through Intelligent Automation

QuadCore is a technology team specialising in AI-driven solutions for the higher education sector. AISAA was built to address a fundamental gap in institutional academic management: the delay between when students begin to struggle and when institutions respond.

The platform combines a deterministic risk evaluation engine, an n8n-powered agentic automation layer, a Groq-powered FAQ chatbot, and a unified multi-role web interface — delivering a complete academic intelligence ecosystem built entirely on open-source and free-tier tools.

Developed as a capstone project at the University of Wollongong in Dubai, AISAA is designed to be immediately deployable at institutional scale with no licensing fees and no dependency on paid ML infrastructure.

🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate preventable academic failure by giving every institution the intelligence and automation needed to act before students reach a critical point of decline.

🔭

Our Vision

A university ecosystem where every stakeholder — from student to Head of Department — operates with complete, real-time academic intelligence and zero administrative overhead.

Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: January 2025 · QuadCore, Dubai, UAE

1

Information We Collect

AISAA collects academic performance data, attendance records, MRP and WAM metrics, student and staff profile information, communication logs, counsellor notes, meeting records, and calendar data — all provided by partnering institutions through a formal data-sharing agreement.

2

How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to generate risk classifications, personalised recommendations, automate communication workflows, coordinate advisor meetings, and support institutional analytics. Personal data is never sold or shared with any third party.

3

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Microsoft Authenticator MFA is enforced for all user roles. Access is strictly role-based via Supabase Row Level Security. All events are logged with timestamps for audit purposes.

4

Data Retention & Deletion

Student data is retained only during the active institutional agreement. Upon termination, all data is securely deleted within 30 days of a written deletion request from the institution.

5

Your Rights

Institutions may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data at any time by contacting aisaaquadcore@gmail.com. All requests processed within 10 business days in compliance with UAE Federal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021).
