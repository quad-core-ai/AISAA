How It Works

From Data to Action — Automatically

AISAA doesn't just report problems. It detects, alerts, and coordinates the entire support response without manual effort.

01

Monitor Performance

AISAA continuously ingests academic data — grades, attendance, assessments — tracking each student's progress in real time.

02

Detect Risk Patterns

Our AI identifies early warning signs, weak subject categories, and predicts academic risk levels before they become failures.

03

Trigger Interventions

The system automatically notifies students and advisors, sending personalized recommendations tailored to each student.

04

Coordinate & Follow Up

Automated workflows schedule meetings, sync calendars, send reminders, and adapt follow-ups continuously.
