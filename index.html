How It Works

From Data to Action — Automatically

AISAA doesn't just report problems. It detects, alerts, and coordinates the entire support response without manual effort.

01

Monitor Performance

AISAA continuously ingests academic data — grades, attendance, assessments — tracking each student's progress in real time.

02

Detect Risk Patterns

Our AI identifies early warning signs, weak subject categories, and predicts academic risk levels before they become failures.

03

Trigger Interventions

The system automatically notifies students and advisors, sending personalized recommendations tailored to each student.

04

Coordinate & Follow Up

Automated workflows schedule meetings, sync calendars, send reminders, and adapt follow-ups continuously.

Information We Collect

AISAA collects academic performance data, attendance records, student and advisor profile information, communication logs, and calendar data — all provided by partnering institutions through a formal data-sharing agreement.

2

How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to generate risk assessments, personalized recommendations, automate communication workflows, and coordinate advisor meetings. We never sell or share personal data with third parties.

3

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Access is strictly role-based, and all access events are logged and monitored. Regular security audits are conducted on the platform.

4
